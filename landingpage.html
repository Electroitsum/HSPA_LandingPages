Learning to Be a Puppy

When 4-month-old Niklaus came to the Houston SPCA, he was suffering from mange and so much neglect he didn't even know how to be a puppy. Thanks to animal lovers like you, his foster family gave him medicated baths for the mange and showed him the joys of puppyhood–dog toys and snuggles. Soon he was ready to fill a new family's home with love and laughter. It's another happy ending that wouldn't have been possible without you!
